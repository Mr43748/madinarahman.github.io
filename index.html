Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta Airport

It is common for many to assume that the busiest airport in the country is located somewhere like New York City. However, the busiest airport in the world is actually located in Atlanta, Georgia. Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport plays a critical role as a connector to a majority of flights through the the United States and the rest of the world. According to a fact sheet provided by Hartsfield-Jackson, "Atlanta is within a two-hour flight for 80 percent of the U.S. population."

Not only does its distance make it the hub for airfare, but the city of Atlanta also attracts many travellers. It is ranked to be the 7th most visited city for business travels, as it is home to over 10 fortune 500 companies. Atlanta is an up and coming bustling city, attracting many all over the country for record breaking attractions. For example, the largest aquarium in the world and home to the world renowned World of Coca-Cola.Further speaking, Atlanta's subtropic weather also adheres to it's business. There are very rare moments, in which extreme conditions will lead to flight delays and cancellations.

Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta Airport continues to win the title for busiest airport, every single year since 1998!
